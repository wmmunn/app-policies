# Privacy Policy — Chromium for GM Blazer EV

**Last updated: June 23, 2026**  
**Developer: William Munn (wmmunn)**

---

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Chromium for GM Blazer EV application ("the App") handles information on your device. This App is a custom-compiled build of the Chromium open-source browser, designed specifically for use on the GM Blazer EV infotainment system.

---

## Information We Collect

### Browsing Data
All browsing data — including history, cookies, cached files, and site data — is stored **locally on your device only**. This data is never transmitted to the developer or any third party.

### Location Data
The App may access your device's location data through the browser geolocation API when a website you visit requests it. Location access is:
- Initiated only by websites you visit, not by the App itself
- Subject to standard browser permission prompts
- Never collected, stored, or transmitted by the developer

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

The developer does not collect, store, transmit, or share:
- Personal identification information
- Browsing history or search queries
- Device identifiers
- Location data
- Any usage analytics or telemetry

---

## Third-Party Services

The App is based on the Chromium open-source browser engine. Websites you visit through the App are governed by their own privacy policies, which are independent of this App and the developer.

---

## Data Security

All data generated by your use of the App remains on your device. The developer has no access to this data and takes no responsibility for data stored locally on your infotainment system.

---

## Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by adults (18 years and older). The developer does not knowingly collect any information from users under 18.
